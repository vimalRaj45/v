About CertifyPro

We're on a mission to simplify certificate creation for organizations worldwide.

Our Story

CertifyPro began as a simple idea inside VSGRPS — a team that constantly built automated tools for colleges, events, and organizations. After working closely with educators, trainers, and institutions, the team noticed one major repeated problem: creating certificates was slow, repetitive, and required too much manual design work.

Instead of relying on Photoshop, Illustrator, or complex design software every time, VSGRPS developed an early version of an automated certificate generator for clients. The response was overwhelming — people needed something fast, simple, accurate, and professional.

This sparked the birth of CertifyPro: a modern, scalable, cloud-based certificate automation platform. Today, we help colleges, educators, event organizers, corporate trainers, and freelancers generate certificates in seconds — with precision, branding, and powerful bulk processing.

From a small internal tool to a full SaaS platform, CertifyPro continues to grow and evolve. Our mission is simple: save time, eliminate repetitive design work, and empower creators to focus on what truly matters — impactful learning and memorable events.
